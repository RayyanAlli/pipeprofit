FAQ

Questions We Get All the Time

Do I need to change my phone number? +
Nope. We port your existing number or set up a new local number — your choice. Your customers keep calling the same number they always have.
How long does setup take? +
Most businesses are live within 72 hours. Our team handles the entire setup — IVR scripting, AI training, and system testing — before you go live.
What if the AI says something wrong? +
We train the AI on your specific services, pricing ranges, and service area. If something needs to be updated, we adjust it — typically within 24 hours.
Is there a contract or lock-in? +
No contracts. All plans are month-to-month. You can cancel anytime with no penalties or fees. We keep you by delivering results, not by locking you in.
What happens when I'm available to take the call? +
The IVR tries to route the call to you first. The AI only kicks in when you or your team can't answer — it's a backup, not a replacement for human contact.
Does this work with my scheduling software? +
Yes. We integrate with most major field service platforms including ServiceTitan, Housecall Pro, and Jobber, as well as Google Calendar. Ask us about your specific tool.
